Residential Power Experts

Safe, Reliable, and High-Performance Electrical Wiring

Your home's electrical panel and infrastructure are the foundation of all your electronics, appliances, and smart tech. We execute clean, master-certified residential electrical services with uncompromising quality.

  • Panel Upgrades & Replacements: Transition your outdated 100A panel to a clean, smart-enabled 200A or 400A service.
  • EV Charger Installations: Dedicated 240V lines and smart chargers for Tesla, Rivian, and all major EV brands.
  • Lighting Design & Power Layouts: Recessed LED retrofitting, dimmers, automated controls, and accent systems.
  • Safety Audits & Code Correction: Troubleshooting electrical faults, grounding issues, and modernizing compliance.

Sustainable Clean Energy

Custom Engineered Solar & Battery Backups

Take absolute control of your utility costs and protect your household against blackouts with a custom designed photovoltaic grid-tied or off-grid solar infrastructure.

  • Tier 1 Solar Modules: High-efficiency micro-inverter arrays that perform in all weather conditions.
  • Home Battery Backups: Integration with Tesla Powerwall, Enphase 5P, and smart transfer switches for seamless off-grid capability.
  • Generator Backup Systems: Standby generator integration with smart automatic transfer switches (ATS) for layered power redundancy.
  • Net Metering Setup: Complete coordination with municipal power grids to sell excess energy back.
  • System Monitoring Apps: Mobile dashboards to watch real-time energy production and home battery storage stats.

Smart Home Architecture

Structured Cabling, High-Speed Data, and Security Networks

A reliable home is built on structured cabling. We install clean, high-bandwidth ethernet, optical fiber, security networks, and audio/video links that bypass congested Wi-Fi airways.

  • Cat6A & Fiber Network Wiring: Fast structured cabling backbones connecting wall outlets, access points, and central media closets.
  • HD IP Security Cameras: Hardwired POE camera integration, network video recorders (NVR), and secure local storage access.
  • Smart Home Automation: Smart switches, intelligent shading, integrated intercoms, and complete home control configurations.
  • Structured AV Closets: Clean equipment rack organization, patch panels, power conditioners, and audio routing setups.
